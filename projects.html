The Projects I used to work on

Gartenservice Hasselbrock und Partner

Branding

Adobe Illustrator

I had the awesome opportunity to shape the branding for 'Hasselbrock und Partner', a newly founded gardening service. As part of the project, I designed the logo and developed the entire corporate identity using Adobe Illustrator. A key focus was to align this branding closely with 'Hasselbrock und Partner' Construction, as these two companies are connected and needed a shared visual style for easy recognition. The result? A fresh simple look that ties the two together, giving the new gardening service a unique identity while keeping that familiar 'Hasselbrock und Partner' vibe!

Bauunternehmen Hasselbrock und Partner

Rebranding

I had the pleasure of creating a new, sleek logo for the construction Company 'Bauunternehmung Hasselbrock und Partner GmbH'. Using Adobe Illustrator, I focused on a clean, minimalist design to reflect the company’s professional and reliable image. App-Concept

Student Project

As part of a three-person team, I worked with the company 'Huth' over a three-month period to develop a concept for a sales-boosting app. Our goal was to create a tool to support sales representatives on-site, specifically in advising customers and closing sales for fencing products. The app concept incorporated features like augmented reality (AR) to provide visual aids that help customers better visualize products in real time. This project allowed us to bridge digital tools with hands-on sales support, creating an innovative solution to enhance the sales process and improve customer experience.
